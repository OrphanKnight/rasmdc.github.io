Mission and Goals

The mission of the IEEE Robotics & Automation Society at Miami Dade College is to expose students to the use and application of technology and circuits in the disciplines of computing and electrical design. Our motto is LEARN, COLLABORATE, CREATE, and HAVE FUN! No prior experience needed.   


Our mission is to encourage participants for all levels to use technology and design skills to collaborate on projects that involves research, design, implementation and management. This club will simulate real world scenarios where teamwork, communication, project management and budget management skills are essential. These skills will be enhanced for every active member while performing every steps for each project with the aim of applying knowledge to real world applications.


Meetings for the 2019 Fall semester are:

* Project Meetings on Mondays & Fridays: 6pm - 7pm
